WHERE TO BUY
Where can I buy your products?
Golf Trends Inc. has been supplying golf equipment to almost all golf course pro shops and golf retail/specialty shops for over 25 years. If you are interested in any of the brands/products we distribute, please contact your local
proshop/retail golf establishment about pricing and availability.
I contacted my local proshop/golf retailer, but they do not have what I'm looking for?
Our product offering is quite large, and it is almost impossible for a proshop/golf retailer to carry every product we offer.
However, every proshop/retailer can place a special order for any product we have in stock. Also, our warehouse staff are second to none - shipping most orders same day if they are received before 12pm EST.
What are your warranty policies?
Golf Trends Inc. is a Canadian distributor of golf products. As a Canadian distributor, we are responsible for covering the warranty claims for all product lines we represent if they are purchased in Canada from an authorized retailer. We are not responsible for warranty claims on products purchased outside of Canada, or purchased second-hand from an unauthorized source.
I purchased a product online from outside of the country, and now have a warranty issue. What should I do?
Golf Trends Inc. is dedicated to supporting the golf pro shops and golf retail stores servicing the millions of golfers playing golf within Canada. Should you require warranty support for a product purchased outside of Canada, you must deal directly with the retailer you purchased the product from.
