About Us
 
Innovation Line (formerly known as Innovation Specialties) has been a supplier in the Promotional Products industry since 1983. From humble beginnings in our Founders’ garage, we have grown into an industry leading supplier with hundreds of employees and a vibrant multi-building campus serving your clients’ promotional marketing needs. Our product line is designed to promote any brand in a sophisticated, professional and yet, economical manner.
 
At Innovation Line, we know the business world is evolving at a rapid rate. To keep pace, we invest a substantial amount of time and money into our people, technology, printing and manufacturing equipment and physical plant infrastructure. We’re proud of the results which include being named a Counselor Magazine Top 10 Fastest Growing Supplier in 2013, 2014, 2015 and 2016. We consistently attain the highest Customer Service, Product Quality and Problem Resolution ratings of A+ and 5 Stars. We are honored by these accolades but nothing gets our team more excited than a thank you email from an appreciative distributor who was able to close a deal due to our creativity and flexibility. Innovation Line understands the challenges distributors face. It’s our mission to ensure your orders are handled in a timely, professional and streamlined manner.
 
Thank you for visiting, we hope to hear from you soon.
 
 
Innovation Line honors the Supplier/Distributor/End-User relationship and does not sell products directly to retail end-user customers.
