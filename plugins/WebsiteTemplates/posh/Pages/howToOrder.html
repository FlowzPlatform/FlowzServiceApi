How to Order

ORDER INFORMATION


Acknowledgement of Orders
All orders must be submitted in writing, either by fax or email.


E-mail: sales@poshxessories.com
Fax: 909.628.8565


All orders are acknowledged via fax or e-mail. Review your acknowledgement carefully and communicate discrepancies immediately. Unless we are notified immediately, we will not be responsible for errors.


Please provide the following to ensure your order goes through smoothly and efficiently:

 

·ASI#, PPAI#, Customer ID, or Contact Name

·P.O. number or previous P.O. number for repeat orders

·Correct item number and color

·Quantity and price

·Art location and size desired

·Decoration method

·Color/ PMS #

·In-hands date

·Bill to/ Ship to address

·Approve paper proof and invoice acknowledgement as soon as possible.

·Artwork Requirement - Please attach your artwork along with your order.


Art Format
Adobe Illustrator (saved as .eps or .ai files) Vector format with all text converted to outlines.


Modification/Changes to Artwork
Artwork requiring modification will be billed at $25.00 (v) per half-hour.

Production Time
Imprinted Items: 5 – 7 days after approval of paper proof or spec sample.
Un-imprinted Items: 24 hours from receipt of purchase order. If the order is received by 2:30PM (PST), can ship same day if requested.

Rush Orders
PoshXessories recognizes that there are times when you need your order fast. That’s why we offer 1-Day, 2-Day, and 3-Day rush service. Availability is based on production capacity. All other standard charges and terms apply.

Un-imprinted Products
Products without an imprint will be billed at EQP price. Orders must be submitted in writing and if received by 2:00 pm (PST) can ship same day if requested. No minimum quantities required.

Less Than Minimum Charge
All imprinted orders require minimum quantities as listed throughout the catalog. Less than minimum orders will be accepted at a charge of $50.00 (v). Absolute minimum is half of first column quantity.

Insertions
Upon request, we will insert your items at a charge of $0.70 (v) per item inserted. Please contact us for quotation on unusual items.

Order Changes
Orders are considered firm and cannot be cancelled without written
authorization from factory. If order is in production, cost will be billed on
the basis of work completed. An increase in order quantity will be treated
as a separate order unless notice is received prior to producing initial
quantity. All order changes must be in writing.


Over or Under Runs
We do our best to ship exact quantities. However we reserve the right to ship and bill to an
allowable over or under shipment up to 5%.


SALES TOOLS


Samples

Sample orders received by 2PM Pacific Standard Time can ship the same day if specified on order and on Posh Xessories’ preferred carrier if shipping method is not given. Orders received after 2PM (PST) will be processed and shipped the next working day.

Posh Xessories will provide up to $6 net worth of samples at no charge with your shipper number. All samples are offered at EQP pricing. Limit three quantity of same item per customer.

To order a sample order, you must have the following information:

·Company name

·ASI/SAGE/PPAI number

·Item Quantity

·Item Number

·Shipping address

·Ship Method

·Shipper account (if available)

·Billing address.

Failure to provide all information may result in sample delays. 


SHIPPING


Shipping Method
Unless otherwise specified, all orders will ship via best method based on number of cartons, weight, destination, and other information available at time of shipment. Please specify the in-hands date if there is a special event. We reserve the right to not be held liable for delays in transit.

Drop and Split Shipments
$7.00(v) plus freight for each drop shipment on the same date. $10.00(v) for each split shipment on different dates. On orders requiring shipment on different dates, the entire order will be billed at the time of the first shipment. All products must be shipped within six months of the initial shipment.

Individual Product Shipments

Shipments that are shipped individually will have a $2.50(v) fee for each product applied, along with freight for each shipment.


Foreign Shipments
$25.00(v) for each location plus freight.

3rd Party Shipments
$18.75(v) if you provide us with an account number for shipping. we may require call tags to be issued in a timely manner to ensure ship date.

FOB Point
Chino, California 91710

Freight Claims
Title passes from the factory upon consignment of merchandise to a carrier. Although Posh Xessories™ can assist you, freight claims should be filed with the carrier within 10 days. Factory cannot be held responsible for freight delays in transit.

 TERMS


Payment Terms
Net 30 days upon credit approval. New accounts will only accept cash, check, wire transfer, and/or VISA or MasterCard payment, including cost, freight and overruns. Past due accounts will be charged at 1.5% per month on unpaid balance.

Returns
Claims must be made in writing. Merchandise should be kept secure pending inspection or return approval. Returns will not be accepted without a return authorization from Posh Xessories. Un-imprinted merchandise authorized for return by the factory is subject to $25.00(x) service charge plus a restocking fee of up to 15% of the net cost of the order. Factory must be notified of intent to return merchandise within 10 days after receipt of order. All returns must be freight prepaid within 30 days of shipment.

Pricing
Prices subject to change after June 30, 2015. Please visit www.poshxessories.com for updated pricing and promotions.

Guarantee
All products are carefully designed and crafted using the finest materials, and covered by a limited three-year warranty which guarantees the product against defects in material and workmanship. It does not cover damage due to accidents, normal wear and tear, or abuse. With care and normal use, our products will provide the recipient with years of satisfaction. Any item found to be defective will be replaced or repaired at factory’s discretion. In our ever-continuing effort to improve our products, we reserve the right to change specifications and price without notice.
