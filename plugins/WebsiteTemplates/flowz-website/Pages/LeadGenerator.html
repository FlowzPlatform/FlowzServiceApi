Frequently Asked Questions

What guarantee do I have that I'll get warm leads?

We guarantee warm leads right to your inbox within the first 30 days or your money back.

How many warm leads can I expect?

You'll get a minimum of 5-15+, but that will increase over time as we fine-tune our targeted campaigns.

What happens after I sign up?

After you sign up, you'll get access to your on-boarding form, where we'll ask you for important details regarding your potential customers. Information like company size, geographic area, industries or verticals.

Can I provide my own lists?

You can certainly provide us with any lists you have, but we also mine contact data based on the target prospects (type of companies) you're looking to get as customers.

How do you manage my campaign for best results?

Once your campaign has started, we continue to feed your campaign with fresh contacts every day, 5 days a week. We also monitor your account daily, to ensure that your campaign runs at optimal parameters.

How do we get the contact data for the prospects?

During our data research process, we use a mix of channels like phone, email, social media, search engines but also our own data platform that is updated daily.

What is the difference between Fractional & Full-Time service?

"Fractional" services are handled by a part-time employee.  These employees work, not only for you but for a number of clients.  They can typically handle 1 file and a few revisions per day which translates to approximately an hour a day, per client, at most. 

"Full-time" services are handled by employees assigned to you 100%.  They do not work for any other clients. The work during your business hours and with a 40 hour work week.

Do I need to register using my credit card?

Due to the number of trials we offer, we want to ensure we provide free trials to serious customers.  When you register, we will ask you for your credit card details.  But your card won’t be charged until after trial period is over.  We encourage you to please try us out on at least 2 jobs during your trial. 

As well, we use Stripe to process credit card transactions, so your details are very safe.  We don't store any credit card information. 

If you happen to decide to cancel just let us know as support@flowz.com, but we know that you're going to love us!
