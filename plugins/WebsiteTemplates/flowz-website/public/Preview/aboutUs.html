Our Story

Flowz is the genesis of nearly 20 years of business ventures. Flowz taps into thousands of employees at our group of companies: Office Beacon, Lux Video , White Label Staffing and others.

Tap Into Our Thousands of Employees

A new way of working is born

We employ thousands of employees, work with an equal number of partners, and serve thousands of clients. Understanding the value of our professional ecosystem, Pranav Dalal, decided to make the entire ecosystem available to businesses of all sizes.

  • Access tremendous skillsets of our 2500+ employees and vendors based all around the world
  • Access our labor capacity where and when you need it
  • Access our 18+ years ofexperience successfully enabling client growth

The Flowz Mission and Vision

Our mission is to become the world’s leading B2B professional services platform.
We accomplish this by positively disrupting the cost and quality of business workflows.
